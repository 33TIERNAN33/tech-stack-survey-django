Welcome to CareTrack

CareTrack is a donation inventory and survivor resource management system designed to help nonprofit organizations track donations, manage inventory, and coordinate resources more efficiently.

The platform replaces spreadsheet-based tracking with a centralized, role-based web application where staff can manage inventory, donors can submit donations, and survivors can browse available resources.

Inventory Management

Staff can track donations, organize storage locations, and assign items to survivors.

Donation Tracking

Donors can submit donation forms and optionally upload images of the items they are providing.

Resource Coordination

Survivors and volunteers can view available inventory and coordinate resource requests.
